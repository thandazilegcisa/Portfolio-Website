My Research Interests:

Gamification:

Whenever i am not working on the web i am either playing or making games. Which is why i have a very keen interest and pursued gamification as a research project. In short Gamification is the use of game elements or design principles in non gaming contexts in order increase engagement and motivation in non-gaming products and services. As such, considering that the web and games have various similarities by virtue of both being multimedia. Gamfication and its potential use cases is something i am excited to get the chance to explore further as a perspective on designing user experiences and interfaces.
